Step 01 • Smart Contract

Company Deposits Funds

Before bounties go live, companies lock their funds in a secure Solana smart contract. This guarantees to hunters that the reward is readily available.

Step 02 • Hunter Action

Bug Submission

Security researchers hunt for vulnerabilities and submit their detailed bug reports securely through the Vulnera dashboard.

Step 03 • Automation

Gemini AI Triage

An integrated AI layer automatically scans the incoming report to filter out spam, low-quality submissions, or duplicates before the company sees it.

Step 04 • Accountability

21-Day Company Review

The company receives the AI-vetted report. A strict 21-day countdown begins, ensuring the researcher receives a timely response.

Resolution Pathways

Approved

The smart contract instantly releases the funds directly to the hunter's crypto wallet.

Rejected

The company denies the bug but is strictly required to provide a clear technical reason.

No Response

If 21 days pass, hunters can flag the company to Admins for platform penalization.
